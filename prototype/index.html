All the world's cities, roads, and rooftops fit in about 3 of these 1,000 tiles — the actually-paved part is roughly one tile. At planet scale, concrete is a rounding error, and a rounding error motivates no one. The lever isn't up here. Switch to the square kilometre you live on.
